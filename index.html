Telephone Answering

Customer Support

Receive unlimited support from our accounts team. Our dedicated staff are available via phone, email, live chat or in person.

We do not automatically renew your service with us. We will contact you near the end of your subscription to ask if you’re happy to renew.

Business Address

When purchasing our Virtual Business Address, you can use our address as your business address wherever you need to, such as on your website and stationery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What features are included with the MYCO app?

A

Our MYCO app allows clients full control over their account, with the ability to upload ID, view scanned mail and read telephone messages moments after they are taken. You can enable push notifications to create an alert to new messages.
The MYCO app is available for free download from the App Store and Google Play.

Q

Where are your office locations?

A

The London Office has three prestigious Central London addresses in the West End, City, and the Strand. We also have further locations in Edinburgh and Ipswich and are aiming to soon open offices in Manchester, Cardiff and Gibraltar.

Q

What services do you provide?

A

We are a business-to-business service provider specialising in affordable and flexible products, including Registered Office Address, Director Service Address, Business Address, Company Formations, Telephone Answering Services and Meeting Room hire in London.

Q

How much do your services cost?

A

We offer a range of affordable, fully-inclusive and flexible packages with no hidden fees. Click here to view our packages and associated costs. We also like to help new clients by offering 10% of all new orders. Simply apply for your discount at the top of the page.

Q

How do I join your Partner Program?

A

We work with many partners who promote our services as their own to generate extra income for their business. Simply complete our contact form to register your interest and we will be in touch.
Click here to discover how our Partner Program works in more detail.

Q

Which payment methods do you accept?

A

We accept payment from most credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard and American Express. We cannot accept cheques or cash.

Q

When was The London Office formed?

A

The London Office, a division of Registered Office (UK) Ltd was formed in late 2014 and has since grown to become the one of the UK’s most trusted behind-the-scenes B2B service providers with nearly 20,000 clients globally.

Q

Your prices are very competitive – are there any hidden costs?

A

We charge £0.20p per item of business mail forwarded to you. Upon signing up to our Virtual Business Address or Virtual Business Plus packages, we ask for a £20.00 postal deposit to cover the cost of postage.

Q

What are your office opening hours?

A

Our offices are open for our friendly and professional support team to work on your behalf from 9am – 5.30pm Monday to Friday (excluding UK public holidays). We are available in person, by telephone, email and live chat during these hours.
