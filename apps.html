Frequently Asked Questions

Read our series of questions that are commonly asked by our clients

Is the MYCO app free?
Yes. Our exclusive MYCO smartphone app is completely free to download and use.
How quickly will I get my telephone messages?
We upload all messages to your account the moment we put down the phone. Enable push notifications in your device settings to ensure you are alerted as soon as you receive them.
What is your app compatibility?
The MYCO app requires iOS 11.0 or later and is compatible with the iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch. For Androids, MYCO requires Android 4.4 or later.
What size is the MYCO app?
Downloading the MYCO app from the App Store requires 37.5MB free space. Downloading it from Google Play requires 9.5MB free space.
