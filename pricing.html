Frequently Asked Questions

Read our series of questions that are commonly asked by our clients

Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept payment from most credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard and American Express. We cannot accept cheques, cash or PayPal payments.
Do you offer any discounts?
Yes. We offer a 10% discount on all orders for new clients, as well as for upgrades for existing clients. We also offer huge discounts when ordering annual services or combined packages.
Why do I need to pay a postal deposit?
For all services that include receiving business mail, we require a £20 postal deposit to cover the cost of forwarding your business mail on to you.
When opting for forwarding, business mail is charged at 20p per letter (plus the cost of forwarding). Once the deposit runs out you will be required to top it back up. A notification will be sent to you when you fall under £5.
How long are your service terms?
Our service terms range from 1 month, 3 months, 6 months up to 12 months.
Individual and combined packages available for 1 month, 3 months, 6 months or 12 months:
- Virtual Business Address
- Virtual Business Plus
- Telephone Answering Service
- Virtual Business Address + Telephone Answering Service
- Virtual Business Plus + Telephone Answering Service
- Virtual Business Plus + Website & Email Hosting
Individual and combined packages only available for 12 months:
- Registered Office Address
- Registered Office Address + Director Service Address
- Virtual Business Plus + Legal Document Pack
I don’t know which service I require. Can you help?
Of course. While we try simplifying our services as much as possible, we realise some terms can seem confusing – especially if you are just starting out.
Our friendly and knowledgeable team at The London Office are happy to help walk you through the order process and answer any questions or queries you have about any of our services.
If I change my mind, can I cancel my service?
Yes. You can change your mind and cancel your service for any reason within 14 days of your order date.
