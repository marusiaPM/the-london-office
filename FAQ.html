Frequently Asked Questions

COVID - 19 Update

From 1st September 2020, all our services will resume as normal. These include meeting room hire and mail collection.

COVID-19 – Business Support

We can not provide advice for businesses affected by COVID-19. Please contact government Business Support for official advice.
If you have a physical business property and would like information regarding business rates relief schemes, you may be able to get business rates relief from your local council to reduce your bill. Please visit gov.uk

COVID-19 – Why haven’t I received my mail?

Due to the current issues surrounding COVID-19, there may be minor delays in receiving your post, especially with post coming from abroad. Our staff are now back in the office and working hard to fulfil all post forwarding services.
If you would like us to open and scan your mail, please email us at post@thelondonoffice.com. Alternatively, login to your account and update your mailing preferences.

COVID-19 – Can I still use the meeting rooms?

We have suspended the use of our meeting rooms. Our regular business services, such as mail forwarding or scan and email, company formation and Telephone Answering are all still available.

Which service do I need?

Each of our individual service and combined package pages explain in detail what each is for and how they work. While we try simplifying our services as much as possible, we realise some terms can seem confusing – especially if you are just starting out.
Our friendly and knowledgeable team at The London Office are happy to help walk you through the order process and answer any questions or queries you have about any of our services.

What ID do I need to provide and where do I upload it?

You are required to supply proof of your ID (passport, driving licence photocard, government-issued ID card, HM Forces ID card, valid Student card, employment ID card, disabled driver blue badge) and proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, mortgage statement, TV licence, HMRC Notice of Coding) within seven days.
You will be shown where to upload your ID once your order is complete.

How long does company formation take?

Once you complete the form on your account, we submit this to Companies House after we have reviewed your submission. If this is submitted during business hours, it may take up to 24 hours for your company to be formed. However, it could take longer.

What is a Postal Deposit?

For all services that include receiving business mail, we require a £20 postal deposit to cover the cost of forwarding your business mail on to you.

When you opt for mail forwarding, your business mail is charged at £0.20p per letter (plus the cost of forwarding)

Once your postal deposit runs out, you are required to top it up. We send you a reminder when your postal deposit falls below £5.00
