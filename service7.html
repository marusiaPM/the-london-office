Frequently Asked Questions

Read our series of questions that are commonly asked by our clients

Where are your meeting rooms?
We have two meeting rooms available for hire – one at 85 Great Portland Street (London) and one at 7 Bell Yard (London).
How many people can your meeting rooms facilitate?
Our meeting room at 85 Great Portland Street (London) can facilitate 4-7 people and our meeting room at 7 Bell Yard (London) can facilitate 6-8 people.
Is there a cost for cancelling a booking on a meeting room?
If your booking is cancelled more than 24 hours beforehand, there is no charge. If it is cancelled within 24 hours of your booking, you will be charged for the booking.
How do I book a meeting room?
To book one of our meeting rooms, simply fill out our contact form and we will get back to you. Alternatively, you can contact us via phone or email. You can also book a meeting room via your account admin panel.
Bookings are not official until you have received a confirmation email from us.
What facilities do you offer at your meeting rooms?
We have contemporary, unbranded meeting rooms available with free WIFI, photocopying and printing at both of our London meeting rooms.
Are refreshments available at your meeting rooms?
Our meeting room at 7 Bell Yard (London) has water, tea and coffee making facilities; however, our meeting room at 85 Great Portland Street has no refreshment facilities available.
What times are your meeting rooms available?
Both of our meeting rooms are available from Monday - Friday, 9:30am - 5:00pm. If a meeting overruns slightly – as they often do – we can be flexible.
