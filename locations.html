Frequently Asked Questions

Read our series of questions that are commonly asked by our clients

What are your office opening times?
Our offices in London, Edinburgh and Ipswich all have the same opening times: 9am – 5.30pm, Monday – Friday (excluding UK public holidays).
Please note, we try to avoid booking meeting rooms or mail collections for 9am because transport can sometimes be unpredictable.
Can I use two different addresses for different services, for example Registered Office Address and Director Service Address?
No. Only one address can be used for combined services.
Can I collect my mail in person?
Yes. All we ask is that you let us know when you are coming in so we can ensure your mail is ready for collection. Mail collection is not available from our 63-66 Hatton Garden (London) or 101 Rose Street South Lane (Edinburgh) offices.
Can I receive parcels?
Parcels are accepted within our business mail service at London W1W, London WC2, Edin-burgh EH3 and Ipswich IP1 locations only. Maximum of 1 per day - maximum 3kg / shoebox size. All parcels must be addressed to the business name. No regular returns accepted. You can arrange a courier to drop off or pick up your parcel, or we can forward it to you same day (up to 2kg).
If parcels are not collected within 2 weeks, they will be disposed of via a confidential waste service.
What are the nearest stations to your Central London offices?
Our 85 Great Portland Street address is located within easy walking distance of Oxford Circus, Goodge Street and Great Portland Street Underground stations.
Our 63-66 Hatton Garden address is a short walk from Farringdon station and Chancery Lane Underground station.
Our 7 Bell Yard address is approximately equidistant from Temple, Holborn and Blackfriars Underground stations.
Will you be opening offices in other locations?
Yes. We are opening more offices in London, Manchester, Cardiff and Gibraltar very soon.
